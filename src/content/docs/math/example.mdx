### Problem 2.3.15 - Solving a quadratic equation

Solve: $ x^2 + 4x + 2 = 8 $

<Solution prompt='Show solution'>

$$ nl, justify = left
    D = b^2 - 4ac = 16 - 8 = 8
    sqrt(D) = sqrt(8)
    x_(1,2) = (-b +- sqrt(D))/(2a) = (-4 +- sqrt(8))/2 = -2 +- sqrt(2)
$$
